About Us

Reimagining Urban Mobility

We improve urban mobility by reducing parking congestion using smart sensors, real-time tracking, and mobile integration — providing seamless parking experiences in crowded cities like Mumbai.

🎯 Mission

To make parking simple, fast, and efficient for every driver.

🌐 Vision

Smart cities with zero parking stress and reduced urban congestion.
